Who We Are

Explore the space & ready to play.

Meta Tech is a high-performance, scalable, EVM-compatible, and secure smart-contract platform. Meta Tech’s mainnet deployment Opera—is built on Meta Tech’s consensus mechanism, Lachesis. Meta Tech is a leaderless, asynchronous, and byzantine fault-tolerant Layer 1 blockchain protocol.

Lachesis enables Meta Tech to deliver fast transaction speeds, low transaction costs, and deterministic finality. This is achieved while remaining permissionless, decentralized, and open-source.
